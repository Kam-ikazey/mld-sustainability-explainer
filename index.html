Technical Framework

Under the hood

The Mylocaldost Sustainability Assessment is a proprietary evaluation matrix designed to quantify the environmental and socio-economic footprint of our hospitality partners. Adapted from the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and localized for the Indian context based on 7 years of operational data, the framework evaluates properties across 7 weighted parameters containing 30 precise indicators.

⚙️ALGORITHM
core_rating = (core_yes / 6) × 2.5
detailed_rating = (detailed_raw / detailed_max) × 2.5
eco_rating = core_rating + detailed_rating

The assessment follows a two-tier model. The 6 core criteria questions contribute up to 2.5 points, and the 28 detailed questions contribute up to 2.5 points. These combine for a final Eco Rating out of 5.0, which is also mapped across our 5 elements.

CO2 Impact Calculation

The science
🌍

Beyond assessment scores, this is how we actually measure the carbon footprint of every stay on Mylocaldost.

For every guest booking, our model answers one question: "How much CO2 did this stay avoid (or produce), and how much does the property's land absorb?" It measures four operational areas and two land-based benefits.

Key Design Choices

Safeguards
01
Caps on Efficiency Claims
Energy efficiency is capped at 70% and water saving at 80% to prevent unrealistic claims.
02
RE Additionality at 50%
Only half of renewable energy use is credited, reflecting the idea that some renewables would exist anyway.
03
Cooking is a Deduction
Unlike other factors (which are avoided emissions), cooking fuel CO2 is subtracted from the total.
04
Trees Reported Separately
Tree sequestration (~345 kg/guest-night) is so large compared to operational savings (~6 kg) that it is shown as a separate land-based metric.
