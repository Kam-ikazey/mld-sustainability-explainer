🌍 CO₂ Sequestered — 46,000 kg ▼
Tree sequestration (Heal Farm):
12,000 trees × 21 kg CO₂/tree/year ÷ 365 = 690 kg/night
60 room-nights × 690 = 41,400 kg

Operational savings (all properties):
1,500 room-nights × ~3 kg avg net savings = 4,500 kg
Net savings include: energy efficiency (20%), renewable energy, water recycling, and composting — minus cooking fuel emissions.

Total: ~46,000 kg HIGH CONFIDENCE

References: IPCC mature tree absorption rate: 21 kg CO₂/year. India CEA grid emission factor: 0.73 kg CO₂/kWh (2023). Waste-to-landfill factor: 1.5 kg CO₂/kg diverted (EPA).
